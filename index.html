URL Shortening Web Service


This website provides a service to transform a long URL(Uniform Resource Locator) to a short alias.

You CAN:

  • Enters a long URL for shortening
  • Customizes your short URL
  • Redirects the short URL to original long URL
  • Keeps track of click data of a short URL

Instructions:

  • First you should write the original URL in the "Input URL" textbox.
  • By clicking "Transform!" button, you will get a shortened URL generated by our service and shown in the "Output URL" textbox.
  • You can also customize your own shortened URL. By writing your customized URL in the "Output URL" text box and clicking the "Customize!" button, your customized URL is confirmed if it hasn't been used.
  • You can only type in letters, numbers and dashes for your customized URL.
